---
The **first delivery** of this year's shelter kits has reached resettled families in Beledweyne. Each kit includes materials for a durable shelter structure, along with basic household items.

Distribution was coordinated with community leaders to make sure kits reached households that had been resettled but were still living in temporary shelter. Field staff will follow up with each household in the coming weeks to confirm the shelters are in place before the rainy season.
